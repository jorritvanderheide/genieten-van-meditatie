How painful it is to be disconnected from yourself.

Even more so if you notice that neither your relationships nor your work are flowing any more either.

When I am connected with myself I feel energetic, alive and fulfilled. I want to share with you what helps me find this connection. 'What contributes to bliss?'

In this booklet you will read that what contributes to bliss is:

  • being with what is
  • having the right attitude towards your partner
  • relaxation
  • a good relationship with your mother
  • going your own way

You will also receive my newsletter - with texts which will give you inspiration for joy in daily life, tips which can help you with difficult situations in life, insight into relationships and notice of upcoming workshops and meditations.

I am looking forward to sharing with you what can help you to live with Heart and Soul.

This booklet also gives you an idea of the background from which I work with people. If this appeals to you, I look forward to meeting you. - Modita
