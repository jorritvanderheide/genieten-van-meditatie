I, Modita van Zummeren, owner of Joy in Meditation take the protection of your data as important. I treat the data with care and respect.

Storage of Personal Data

Newsletter

In my database are email addresses of people who have signed up for the newsletter. The email addresses are only used for sending the newsletter and never provided to others. Would you not like to receive the newsletter anymore then at the bottom of each newsletter you find the possibility to unsubscribe.

Access data

Only I, Modita van Zummeren, have access to the personal data.

Delete your data

You can always send an e-mail to info@genietenvanmeditatie.nl with the request to remove your data or part of them from my database.

Complaint

If you have a complaint about my privacy policy, please send an e-mail to info@genietenvanmeditatie.nl. Information about the AVG can be found at the Authority for Personal Data.
