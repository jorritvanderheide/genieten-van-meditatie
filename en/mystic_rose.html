Osho® Mystic Rose

This is the most beautiful and profound process that I know. A process that opens your heart and releases the fragrance of the rose of your being... Everyone should do it at least once in their lives because it brings you to your core. Words fail to describe it, because it is a wordless process, pure, and only from your heart and your being.

    A taster: Sat evening April 4 and June 6 19:30 - 22:00 ~ €20,00 The full Mystic Rose: 21 consecutive evenings from 2 to 22 Aug 2021, 18:45 - 22:00 ~ €600,00 In dansstudio Laetana, Hoogstraat 105A Eindhoven

The Osho Mystic Rose is a process that cleanses and purifies you , by allowing you to cry all the tears that you never cried as well as by opening you to experience your life’s fullness through laughing abundantly and being able to go completely wild, to be uninhibited. After this, it is very easy to reach the silence which is inside yourself and to experience who you essentially are.

7 evenings, 3 hours of laugher ~ 7 evenings, 3 hours of crying ~ 7 evenings, 3 hours witnessing in silence

You start experiencing yourself and everything around you as so pure, and in such a new way. The quality of this cannot be described in words.

Where nowadays can you totally let go, be exactly as you are? It is possible in this precious process of the Mystic Rose.

Osho

Osho: "All that this world needs is a good cleansing of the heart and all the inhibitions of the past. Laughter and tears can do both. Tears will take out all the agony that is hidden inside you and laughter will take all that is preventing your ecstasy. Once you have learned tha art you will be immensely surprised: why has this not been told up to now? There is a reason: nobody has wanted humanity to have the freshness of a roseflower and the fragrance and the beauty."
