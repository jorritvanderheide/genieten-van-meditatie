About Modita van Zummeren

During my depression, I thought that I would never be well again. The therapy, workshops and trainings that I followed had no lasting results. And then, after a whole lot of trying, I gained profound insights that lifted me out of my depression and my eating disorders.

An unbelievable amout of joy and energy where now at my disposal. I let me fell with my belly on the ground, embraced the earth, and decided to share everything that helped me to live fully again.

Everything I learned can be found in the offer on my website, my book about depression, the epsisodes of my podcast 'From Pain to Being', and my posts on Facebook, Instagram and YouTube.
