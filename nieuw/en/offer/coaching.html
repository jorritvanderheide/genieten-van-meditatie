Coaching

In the personal coaching sessions I facilitate, we go to the core of your problems.

I guide you towards more insight into your problem and also work body-oriented, using the wisdom of your body, which is your purest feedback instrument.

Using the books I have written about depression and eating problems, I will offer an 8-week long counceling trajectory with practical and insightful exercises. I also offer per-hour consultations.

Online course 'Beyond Eating Problems'

De online course 'Beyond Eating Problems'is an 8-week course with practical exercises and meditations that will help you make your eating problems obsolete.

Online course 'Beyond Depression with Consciousness'

De online course 'Beyond Depression with Consciousness' is an 8-week course with practical exercises and meditations that will help you get out of your depression.

My consults take place online (via Zoom) or at my home address in Eindhoven.

The first consult takes half an hour, and is free of charge. In this session, we will find out together how I can help you best. Afterwards, I charge €65,- per hour.
